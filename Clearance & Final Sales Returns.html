Clearance & Final Sales Returns

Due to the nature of clearance product, returns will be limited to defective product upon customer receipt. Returns must be received by AtBatt.com at the address above within 14 days of the customer receipt date. Refunds will not be authorized after 14 days. Before returning any defective item, you must submit an online Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) request form. No returns will be accepted without an RMA number. The RMA number is good for only one (1) item and must match the product authorized for return. RMA numbers are valid for 5 business days. Due to limited stock, replacement product may not be available and cannot be guaranteed.
