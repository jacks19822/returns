Defective Items

Defective items can be returned for a refund during the first 30 days. After the 30 days, the product can be returned, at AtBatt.com's discretion, for: store credit, replacement, exchange or repair.

After 30 days from your invoice date your purchase falls under a warranty replacement. Following the 30 days, you are only eligible for a replacement of the defective item. Customers are responsible for return shipping on warranty items.

Warranty is void if the warranty sticker has been removed or if any internal component has been altered or modified.
