Returns for a Refund

Not happy with your product you purchased from us? No Worries!

Products must be returned within 30 days of the delivery date for a refund. All we ask is that you return the product in "as-new" condition, in original packaging and with all warranty cards, manuals and accessories. We will not accept partial returns. To avoid any delays please make sure the RMA number is visible on the outside of the package.

Customers are responsible for return shipping cost, and a 15% restocking fee may also apply.
